Explore London

The London Eye rises 135 metres above the River Thames, providing panoramic views of the city’s landmarks—including Big Ben, the Houses of Parliament, Buckingham Palace, The Shard and St Paul’s Cathedral.

The Tower of London, a former royal residence, prison and treasury, now houses the Crown Jewels and exhibitions on a thousand years of history. Nearby, the Tower Bridge dazzles visitors with its iconic twin towers and high-level walkways.

Don’t miss Buckingham Palace and St Paul’s Cathedral—two of London's most iconic cultural sites!
