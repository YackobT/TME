Milan – Fashion & Culture Capital

Where modern fashion meets timeless heritage and art.

Top 5 Must-See Places

  • Duomo di Milano – Breathtaking Gothic cathedral and rooftop views.
  • Sforzesco Castle – A Renaissance fortress with museums.
  • The Last Supper – Leonardo da Vinci’s masterpiece (reservation needed).
  • Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II – Elegant 19th-century shopping arcade.
  • Brera Art Gallery – Italian masterpieces and a charming neighborhood.
