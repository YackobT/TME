Rome – Eternal City

Step into ancient history with every corner you turn in the heart of the Roman Empire.

Top 5 Must-See Places

  • Colosseum – The iconic Roman amphitheater.
  • Vatican City – Home to St. Peter’s Basilica and the Sistine Chapel.
  • Trevi Fountain – Throw a coin and make a wish.
  • Roman Forum – Ancient ruins from the empire’s center.
  • Pantheon – A marvel of Roman architecture still standing strong.
