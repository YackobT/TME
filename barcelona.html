Barcelona Must-See Spots

Sagrada Família

Gaudí’s masterpiece with breathtaking architecture and stained glass.

Park Güell

Colorful mosaic-covered park with whimsical designs and city views.

Gothic Quarter

Wander narrow medieval streets and explore old cathedrals and cafes.

Casa Batlló

A surreal Gaudí house with dragon-like rooftop and artistic facade.

Barceloneta Beach

Relax or bike along the city’s iconic seaside neighborhood.
