Barcelona

Art & Architecture

Barcelona is famed for the imaginative architecture of Antoni Gaudí. Explore the surreal Sagrada Familia, a basilica over a century in the making, and the colorful mosaics of Park Güell.

Beaches & Boulevards

From the bustling La Rambla to the laid-back Barceloneta beach, Barcelona offers vibrant street life, tapas bars, and stunning sea views. It’s a city of energy, sunlight, and spirit.

Culture & Catalonia

Visit the Gothic Quarter, Picasso Museum, and catch a flamenco show or a Barça football match. Barcelona celebrates its Catalan heritage with pride and color at every turn.
