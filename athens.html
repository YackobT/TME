Athens

Ancient Wonders

The Acropolis stands high above Athens, a symbol of democracy and ancient architecture. Visit the Parthenon and the new Acropolis Museum nearby.

Modern City Life

Wander the streets of Plaka, try local souvlaki, and explore the Monastiraki flea market. Athens blends the old and new seamlessly.
