Florence – Birthplace of the Renaissance

Discover art, architecture, and culture in Tuscany’s historic capital.

Top 5 Must-See Places

  • Duomo (Cathedral of Santa Maria del Fiore) – The iconic red dome.
  • Uffizi Gallery – World-class art museum with Botticelli & da Vinci.
  • Ponte Vecchio – A picturesque medieval stone bridge with shops.
  • Palazzo Vecchio – Florence’s historic town hall.
  • Piazzale Michelangelo – Sweeping views of Florence from above.
