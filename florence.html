Florence

Renaissance Glory

Florence, the cradle of the Renaissance, is an open-air museum. Admire masterpieces at the Uffizi Gallery and gaze up at Brunelleschi’s dome from the Piazza del Duomo.

Walkable Elegance

Cross the Ponte Vecchio, wander through cobbled streets, and stumble upon artisan shops and gelaterias tucked behind every corner.

Florentine Flair

Dive into Tuscan cuisine with hearty ribollita soup, bistecca alla fiorentina, and regional wines in historic trattorias.
